My Street Code

We believe in using mobile technology to break barriers between each other.

Most importantly, we're determined to giving a hand when needed. Our mission is to make it easier to help homeless individuals. We've developed a quick and secure way to allow people to donate money to homeless individuals by scanning unique QR codes that can be printed on cards and posters. Each QR code is linked to a debit account which the individual can use to pay for necessities. Scan. Share. Love.
